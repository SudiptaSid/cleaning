What Are We?

We are not a cleaning agency or cleaning company, we're an intermediary platform that helps match customers like you with self-employed cleaners. Once you have booked a cleaner you should arrange with them what specific cleaning tasks you would like them to undertake, for example ironing or oven cleaning.
